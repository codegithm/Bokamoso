Business Compliance Workshop

Business compliance training focusses on the basic and minimum business compliance requirements namely:

  • Business registration with the CIPC and filling of annual returns
  • Trademark registrations - protecion of your intellectual property
  • Tax complains, how to register on eFilling and view your compliance status.
  • Registration with labour for UIF & CIOD
  • Preparation of a business plan. Why it is important
  • Understanding the POPI Act(Protection of Personal Information) requirements for your business.
  • BBBEE affidavit - how to obtain it

The business compliance training is aimed at bringing awareness for the enterpreneur on the things that he must be compliant with to avoid penalties and getting into trouble with the authorities. Government will not support any non-compliant buusinesses.

Financial Management Workshop

Financial Management(FM) is a very broad topic in our training we focus on the five following elements namely

  • Bdgeting and forcasting - teaching the basic principle of creating a budget and a forcast. Tracking actual performance against the budget. Using the budget as part of strategic planning and goal setting
  • Cost Analysis - teaching the essence of cost analysis and its importance in the determination of selling price, mark up and gross profit margin. So, to ensure your pricing is competitive i.e, no over or undercharging of goods and services.
  • Sales Analysis - analysing sales to identiy trends, business perforance(growth, regrssion or stagnation) and product perfomance.
  • Cash management - importance of having a policy for cash management, credit sales, supplier payment and petty cash. Learn \ how to prepare cashflow forecast.
  • Financial Reporting - assist enterpreneurs to understand commonly used business and acconting terms to enable the to easily understand ad inteprete financial reports especially the annual financial statement.

Every decision you make or fail to make has financial implications thus need to ensure as an enterpreneur you understand these principles on financial management and their implications for your business
