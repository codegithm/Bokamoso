BUSINESS COMPLIANCE

Business compliance workshop focusses on the basic and minimum business compliance requirements namely:

  • Business registration with the CIPC and filling of annual returns
  • Trademark registrations - protection of your intellectual property
  • Tax complains, how to register on eFilling and view your compliance status.
  • Registration with labour for UIF & CIOD
  • Preparation of a business plan.
  • Why it is important Understanding the POPI Act(Protection of Personal Information) requirements for your business.
  • BBBEE affidavit - how to obtain it

The business compliance workshop is aimed at bringing awareness for the entrepreneur on the things that he must be compliant with to avoid penalties and getting into trouble with the authorities. Government will not support any non-compliant buusinesses.

Investors Readiness Assessments

An investor readiness assessment is a crucial step for entrepreneurs and businesses looking to attract investment from potential investors. It involves evaluating various aspects of your business to determine if business can secure funding. We assist both the investee and capital providers (investor) when it comes to investor readiness. For the capital providers we do lead generations whilst assisting the investee with the following.

  • Financial Projections and Cash Flow Forecasting.
  • Review of Current and Historic Financial Performance.
  • Review of Business Plan.
  • Review of Back Office Support Functions.
  • Review of Business Operations.
  • Provide Investee with Investor Options.
  • Pitch Deck Preparations.

An investor readiness assessment is an ongoing process, and it's essential in identify and address any weaknesses or gaps identified in the business strategy.
