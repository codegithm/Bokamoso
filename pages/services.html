OUR SERVICES

Bokamoso Advisory Services(Bokamoso) is a professional service provider in the field of accounting. Our target market are small and medium enterprises including Non- Profit Organisations(NPOs).

Back Office Support

The services we offer include the following:

  • Financial and management accounting,
  • Internal and external audits,
  • Preparation of monthly, quarterly, and annual financial statements and management accounts,
  • Enterprise risk management,
  • Formulation of internal controls,
  • Bookkeeping,
  • Payroll (issuing payslips and filling EMP201 with SARS)
  • Tax matters,
  • Company registration & filling of annual returns,
  • Financial reporting and financial management/workshop,
  • Business compliance workshop.

Bokamoso works with the latest available accounting package that makes use of cloud services, which makes it very affordable for our clients. Cloud services make it easy for us to connect and work anywhere at any time, giving our clients effecient and effective services. Bokamoso is managed by a SAICA accredited chartered accountant.
